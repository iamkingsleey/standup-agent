How it works

Up and running in minutes

1

Add to Slack

Click "Add to Slack" and install the bot in your workspace via OAuth. Takes 30 seconds.

2

Connect Google Calendar

The bot DMs you a personal calendar link. Click it, approve permissions, and your calendar is linked. Every team member can connect their own.

3

Connect Jira (optional)

Add your Jira credentials in settings, then DM the bot your Atlassian email. Your open tickets appear in every morning standup.

4

Start your day smarter

At 9 AM your personalised standup arrives automatically. DM the bot anything ‚Äî it knows your work context and responds accordingly.
